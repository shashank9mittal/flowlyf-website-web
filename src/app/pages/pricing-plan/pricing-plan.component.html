FAQ

What’s the difference between buying and subscribing ?
When a customer purchases the product from FlowLyf, they pay the full amount of product at the check-out and become the owner of the product. Whereas subscription is a pay per use model, in this the customer pay according to the usage of the product usually a monthly recurring amount.
What subscription plan does Flowlyf offer?
Currently Flowlyf offers subscription for its product FlowDesk Classic and FlowDesk Max. The minimum time frame the FlowDesk can be subscribed for is 3 month. There is no maximum tenure, you cancel the subscription at any time during the tenure by giving 1 month prior notice. Customer can also avail Subscribe- to-Purchase. For more info please read the section below
What is Subscribe-to-Own?
Subscribe-to-Own is a program designed specifically to provide highest degree of flexibility to our customers. You can try our products by subscribing to them and if you love them, purchase them from us at special prices that are transparent and pre-defined, we will subtract what you have paid already. Here is how it works Suppose you Subscribed to the FlowDesk at Rs. 2000/month. After 6 months of the subscription duration, you decide to own it. By this time, you must have already paid Rs. 2,000*6 = Rs. 12,000. And the Adjusted Buying Price of the month is Rs.30000. therefore, Rs. 30,000 - Rs. 12,000 = Rs. 18,000. All you have to do is pay the price Rs. 18,000/- and the FlowDesk is yours! On order page in your profile, you can see the predefined Subscribe -to- Own prices. Decide if you want to Own, and we’ll handle the rest.
How do I opt for Subscribe -to- Own?
If you have subscribed to any of our products for more than 1 month you have unlocked the option to own the product, you shall find own now button on the orders page in your profile. Simply click it and follow the instructions Each product eligible for Subscribe -to- Own shall carry the Adjusted Buying Price for that month. You can choose the option to pay the balance amount and own the product right there.
Why do I have to pay a security deposit?
The deposit is collected to give us some security coverage for the products. This amount is completely refundable if the item is returned without any damages physical and functional.
What if I damage the product?
If the damage caused to the product is repairable, then the cost of repair will be charged and deducted from your security deposit. If non repairable, then you will have to pay the full cost of the product.
